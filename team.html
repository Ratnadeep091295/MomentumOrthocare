Our Team

Dr. Santosh Kumar

MBBS, Diploma in Orthopedics, M.Ch - Orthopedics
Consultant Orthopedic & Joint Replacement Surgeon, Kolkata
Previously Orthopedic Surgeon JIPMER Hospital
Directorate General Of Health Service Govt. Of India
Trained in Computer Navigated Joint Replacement from Asculap Academy, Germany
Director Asculap Academy Asia Pacific Surgeon Training
Certificate of International Excellence in Computer Assisted Joint Replacement
President of Poorva International Orthopedic Foundation

He was educated in one of the most prestigious medical schools, recognized all over the globe,the Jawaharlal Institute of PostGraduate Medical Education and Research (JIPMER), under the government of India, both his graduation and post graduation. During this time he had been assisting his head Prof Dilip K Patro and learnt his basics of orthopedics under him.

He took part actively in the academics, journal clubs and managed the Bone Bank of the JIPMER hospital.

He was trained in joint replacements at the MAX Hospitals, New Delhi. He had been trained in complex and revision joint replacements by the then delta foundation. He was trained in Phoenix, USA by the KLEOS foundation and was trained in Vienna Austria for computer navigated knee replacements and minimally invasive knee replacements.

He held the first computer navigated joint replacement congress in Eastern India on 28th January, 2013 at the Bellevue Clinic Kolkata.

Dr Santosh Kumar also works in the field of arthroscopic surgery and spine surgery. He has done more than five thousand knee replacement surgeries till now.
